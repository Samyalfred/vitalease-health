The Secret Wonders of Paragis Grass Extract

Paragis (Eleusine indica) is a humble grass found in fields, but it hides a powerful secret: it's packed with health benefits! This ancient herbal remedy has been used in traditional medicine for generations — and now it's making a comeback.

🌿 Health Benefits of Paragis Extract

  • ✔️ Powerful Antioxidant
  • ✔️ Natural Detoxifier
  • ✔️ Aids in Lowering Blood Pressure
  • ✔️ Helps Manage Diabetes
  • ✔️ Anti-inflammatory Properties
  • ✔️ Relieves Headaches and Fever

🥤 How to Prepare Paragis Extract

  1. Harvest fresh Paragis grass (clean and green blades).
  2. Rinse thoroughly with clean water.
  3. Cut into small pieces and boil in 1 liter of water for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Let it cool, then strain the liquid.
  5. Store in a clean container — drink 1 glass a day (morning or evening).

📌 Tip:

For better taste, you can add honey or lemon!
