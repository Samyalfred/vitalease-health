5 Foods That Naturally Boost Your Immunity

Your immune system is your body’s first line of defense against infections. To keep it strong, include these 5 natural immunity-boosting foods in your daily diet:

1. Citrus Fruits

Loaded with vitamin C, citrus fruits like oranges, lemons, and grapefruits help stimulate white blood cell production.

2. Garlic

Garlic has powerful anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties. It’s great in soups and stir-fries.

3. Ginger

Ginger helps reduce inflammation and oxidative stress. It’s especially effective when consumed as tea.

4. Spinach

Spinach is packed with vitamin C and antioxidants. Light cooking enhances its nutrient absorption.

5. Yogurt

Yogurt provides probiotics that support a healthy gut — the foundation of a strong immune system.
