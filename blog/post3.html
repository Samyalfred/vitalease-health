🌿 The Secret Wonders of Paragis Grass Extract

Published by Vitalease Health | Written by Samuel Alfred Ekanem

Paragis grass, also known scientifically as Eleusine indica, is a wild grass traditionally used in various indigenous and tropical cultures for its powerful medicinal properties. Also known as "Goosegrass" or "Asparagus grass" in some regions, it grows abundantly in tropical countries and is increasingly gaining attention in modern herbal circles for its remarkable healing benefits.

📜 A Glimpse into Ancient Healing

For centuries, our ancestors—especially during the early centuries—relied on the healing properties of plants. Paragis was one of the most respected herbs used for treating fever, infections, swelling, and even spiritual cleansing. In ancient Philippine and African traditional medicine, it was used to detoxify the blood, reduce internal heat, and heal inflammatory diseases.

🍵 How to Prepare Paragis Tea or Extract

➡️ Method 1: Paragis Herbal Tea

  1. Gather 1 cup of freshly harvested Paragis grass (with roots).
  2. Wash thoroughly with clean water.
  3. Boil in 3–4 cups of water for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Strain and drink 1–2 cups daily. Add honey or lemon for flavor if needed.

➡️ Method 2: Paragis Extract

  1. Wash and chop fresh Paragis leaves and stems.
  2. Blend with 1 cup of filtered water.
  3. Strain and store in a glass container.
  4. Consume 2 tablespoons daily for detox or as directed by a herbalist.
